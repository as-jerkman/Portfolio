About

Hi!

My name is Josh.

I am originally from Evansville, Indiana but moved to the Indianapolis area about 6 years ago when I met my wife.

I enjoy spending time with family and friends, spoiling my miniature dachshunds, keeping my lawn to perfection, and playing darts.

The majority of my work history has been spent in the construction industry. I decided that I wanted to pursue something that I was interested in and went back to school at Ivy Tech in March of 2020 with a major in Software Development. I made the switch to Eleven Fifty Academy in November of 2020, and I can confidently say that this career transition is the right choice for me. I love software development.

I consider myself to be a logical and literal thinker. I am great at problem-solving and enjoy rising up to conquer challenges. I love learning new things, and in this field, I have learned there is no limit on knowledge. I like to work with people, network, and build relationships. I am determined, focused, and hard-working.

I cannot wait to see where software development takes me. The future is looking bright.
